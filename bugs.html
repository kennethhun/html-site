Shoes for Bugs

While our previous startup, Shirts for bugs, was a failure we are confident Shoes for bugs will succeed!

Honestly, who would have thought making bug inclusive shirts would be so difficult?

The benefits of expanding into the bug market

  • Bugs have far more legs than most creatures on our planet
    • The humble millipede would generate plenty of profits per customer
    • Spiders may also be profitable clients
  • Bugs have large families
    • A good shoe for ants may bring a wealth of potential customers, far more than any human market

Issues to be addressed

  • Unforunately, the world has yet to recognize the currencies used by insects
  • We also acknowledge the potential of pollution, however we believe that insects simply need to learn to recycle
